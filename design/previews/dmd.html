levels

Scenes are authored at 128×32 logical resolution in exactly these 5 dot states — no intermediate colors. Lit dots get a bloom halo ≈40% of dot diameter, applied by the runtime LED shader: bake plain levels, never pre-bloomed art. Score/status lines use the DIGITS 5×7 and SMALL 3×5 bitmap fonts (5×7 shown). Baked to sprite-sheet frames at build time (plan §5c); never live SVG/DOM on the grid.
