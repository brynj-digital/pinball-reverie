Display — the “chrome horizon” recipe

Vertical chrome gradient with a hard stop at the horizon (46→52%) · 3 mm ink outline under the fill (paint-order: stroke) · 1 mm neon rim in the table's feature color · soft violet glow behind. Final wordmarks are hand-drawn letterforms using this recipe — the monospace face here is a stand-in showing treatment, not the letterforms.

Rules

1 · Wordmarks/logos: drawn SVG letterforms per table, chrome-horizon treatment.
2 · UI text: the pixel-grotesk voice above; never anti-aliased serif or humanist faces.
3 · DMD text: bitmap fonts only — DIGITS 5×7, SMALL 3×5 (see the DMD card). Never vector type on the dot grid.
