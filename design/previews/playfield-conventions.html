Playfield conventions

The SVG is the single source of truth for table shape — art and collision in one file.

Lower playfield excerpt (master mm units) — collision · sensor · anchor over dimmed art
Layer id prefixMeaning
collision-wall-<name>solid open chain (path centerline)
collision-loop-<name>solid closed chain
sensor-<kind>-<name>scoring zone, non-solid → EventBus
kinds: drain · ramp-entry · ramp-exit · rollover · lane · kicker · spinner · target
anchor-<entity>[-<which>]circle center places a code-defined body
required: flipper-left · flipper-right · plunger · spawn
art-… / unprefixedvisual only — parser ignores

Units: 1 SVG user unit = 1 mm; playfield viewBox 0 0 520 1050; the parser divides by 1000 — no other scale anywhere. Curves flatten to ≤1 mm chord error.

Gap rules (from the physics): every gap between collision surfaces is <13.5 mm (ball can't enter) or >38 mm (passes freely) — between wedges the ball. Inlane guides end tangent to the flipper base circle (r 12 mm at the anchor), past its apex. Verify every table against the in-game debug overlay before shipping art.
